## Overview
The Unique Disability ID (UDID) Card is a government-issued identification document for persons with disabilities (PwDs) in India. It creates a national database for PwDs and acts as a single, pan-India document for identification and verification, replacing the need to carry multiple certificates.

## Benefits
- **Unified Access:** Simplified access to various government schemes, including scholarships, pension schemes, and skill training programs.
- **Concessions:** Easier access to travel concessions (railway/bus), subsidized assistive devices, and other public services.
- **Portability:** The card is valid across all states and Union Territories in India.
- **Efficiency:** Digitized records and a QR code system for quick authentication, reducing paperwork and preventing fraud.
- **Inclusion:** Facilitates better access to education, healthcare, and employment opportunities.

## Eligibility criteria
- Any Indian citizen with a disability recognized under the **Rights of Persons with Disabilities (RPwD) Act, 2016**.
- Typically requires a **benchmark disability of 40% or more**.
- No age limit; children, adults, and senior citizens are all eligible.

## Required documents
1. **Aadhaar Card** (for identity/address proof).
2. **Passport-size photograph**.
3. **Disability certificate** (if already issued).
4. **Relevant medical reports** (for new assessments).

## How to apply
1. **Online Registration:** Visit the [Swavlamban Card website](https://www.swavlambancard.gov.in/) and click on "Apply for Disability Certificate & UDID Card".
2. **Fill Details:** Enter personal, contact, and disability details in the online form.
3. **Upload Documents:** Upload scanned copies of the Aadhaar card, photograph, and existing disability certificate (if any).
4. **Medical Assessment:** Choose a medical authority/government hospital for assessment. You will be scheduled for an evaluation by a medical board to determine the type and percentage of disability.
5. **Verification & Issuance:** After verification and assessment approval, the UDID card is generated. It can be downloaded digitally, and a physical copy is sent via Speed Post to the registered address.

## Tracking Application
Applicants can track their application status using their login credentials on the official portal.

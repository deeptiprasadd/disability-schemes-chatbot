## Overview
The National Institute for Empowerment of Persons with Multiple Disabilities (Divyangjan) is a national resource center established in 2005 to serve as a resource center for empowerment of persons with Multiple Disabilities. The institute provides various services and support for persons with disabilities, including education, training, and employment opportunities. The institute is under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (Divyangjan), Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Govt. of India.

## Benefits
- Education and training opportunities for persons with multiple disabilities
- Employment opportunities for persons with disabilities
- Support and resources for persons with multiple disabilities

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with multiple disabilities, as defined by the RPWD Act (2016) and The National Trust (1999) Act
- Children with multiple disabilities who require early intervention and support

## Required documents
1. Proof of disability
2. Proof of age and identity

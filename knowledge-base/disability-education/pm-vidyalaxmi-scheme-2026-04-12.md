## Overview
The PM-VIDYALAXMI SCHEME is a scheme that provides support for education and vocational training for persons with disabilities. The scheme aims to promote inclusive education and provide opportunities for persons with disabilities to acquire skills and knowledge.

## Benefits
- Financial support for education and vocational training
- Opportunities for skill development and employment

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with disabilities, as defined by the RPWD Act (2016) and The National Trust (1999) Act
- Students with disabilities who require financial support for education and vocational training

## Required documents
1. Proof of disability
2. Proof of age and identity
3. Proof of income and financial need

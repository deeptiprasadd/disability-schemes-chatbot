## Overview
The National Overseas Scholarship Scheme (NOS) for ST candidates is a scholarship scheme for Scheduled Tribe (ST) candidates who are pursuing higher education abroad.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for ST candidates pursuing higher education abroad
- Opportunity to study in a foreign university and gain international experience

## Eligibility criteria
- ST candidates who are pursuing higher education abroad
- Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria set by the scheme

## Required documents
1. ST certificate
2. Passport
3. Admission letter from the foreign university

## How to apply
Step-by-step instructions are not explicitly mentioned on the website, but candidates can apply through the NIEPID website or by contacting the institute directly.

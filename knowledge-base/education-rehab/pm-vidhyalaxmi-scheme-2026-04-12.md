## Overview
The PM-Vidhyalaxmi Scheme is a scheme that provides financial assistance to students who are pursuing higher education.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for students pursuing higher education
- Opportunity to study in a university or institution of their choice

## Eligibility criteria
- Students who are pursuing higher education
- Candidates who meet the eligibility criteria set by the scheme

## Required documents
1. Admission letter from the university or institution
2. Income certificate

## How to apply
Step-by-step instructions are not explicitly mentioned on the website, but candidates can apply through the NIEPID website or by contacting the institute directly.

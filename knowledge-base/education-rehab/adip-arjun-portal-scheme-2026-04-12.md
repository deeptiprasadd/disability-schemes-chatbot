## Overview
The ADIP Arjun Portal Scheme is a part of the Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids/Appliances (ADIP) scheme, which aims to provide aids and appliances to persons with disabilities.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for the purchase and fitting of aids and appliances
- Access to a wide range of aids and appliances through the ADIP Arjun Portal

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with disabilities who are eligible for ADIP scheme benefits

## Required documents
1. Disability certificate
2. Income certificate

## How to apply
Step-by-step instructions are not explicitly mentioned on the website, but candidates can apply through the ADIP Arjun Portal or by contacting the NIEPID directly.

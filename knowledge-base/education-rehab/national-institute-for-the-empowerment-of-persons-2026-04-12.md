## Overview
The National Institute for the Empowerment of Persons with Intellectual Disabilities (NIEPID) is an autonomous body under the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities, Ministry of Social Justice & Empowerment, Government of India. NIEPID provides quality services to persons with intellectual disabilities in the national interest. The institute offers various schemes, including education, rehabilitation, and employment opportunities.

## Benefits
- Education and training programs for persons with intellectual disabilities
- Rehabilitation services, including respite care and family cottage services
- Employment opportunities through various posts at NIEPID and its regional centers

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with intellectual disabilities
- Candidates who have applied for various posts at NIEPID and its regional centers

## Required documents
1. Life certificate form for pensioners of NIEPID
2. Mandatory course completion and comprehensive assessment on the iGOT Karmayogi platform for employees

## How to apply
Step-by-step instructions are not explicitly mentioned on the website, but candidates can apply for various posts and schemes through the NIEPID website or by contacting the institute directly.

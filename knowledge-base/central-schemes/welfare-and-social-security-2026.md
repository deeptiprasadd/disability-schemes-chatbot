## 1. ADIP Scheme
**Full Name:** Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids & Appliances.
ADIP helps PwDs acquire essential certified aids and appliances to enhance mobility and independence.
- **Coverage:** Wheelchairs, calipers, hearing aids, visual aids, prosthetics, and pre-aid surgeries.
- **Eligibility:** Indian citizens with at least 40% disability and meeting specific income thresholds.

## 2. DDRS (Deendayal Disabled Rehabilitation Scheme)
A cornerstone program that funds NGOs and voluntary organizations working for PwDs.
- **Focus:** Financial grants for special schools, vocational training centers, and early intervention centers.
- **Scope:** Infrastructure improvements (ramps, accessible toilets) and rehabilitation services.

## 3. Indira Gandhi National Disability Pension Scheme (IGNDPS)
Part of the National Social Assistance Programme (NSAP), offering monthly financial aid to vulnerable adults.
- **Grant Amount:** Approx. ₹300/month (base central contribution, often topped up by states) for those aged 18-79. Higher amounts for those 80+.
- **Eligibility:** 80% or above disability and belonging to a Below Poverty Line (BPL) household.

## 4. General Welfare Benefits
The government offers several additional welfare incentives for social and economic inclusion:
- **Marriage Incentive:** One-time financial support (e.g., ₹30,000) for PwDs upon marriage.
- **Maintenance Grant:** Monthly allowance (e.g., ₹1,500) for individuals with specific severe disabilities.
- **Educational Scholarship:** Annual support (ranging from ₹3,000 to ₹8,000 or more).
- **Travel Concessions:** Free or subsidized public transport (Railways/State Buses) for valid disability certificate holders.
- **Unemployment Allowance:** Small monthly stipend (e.g., ₹400–₹1,000) for unemployed PwDs with 10th-grade education, aged 18-48.

## Application Documents
Common requirements across these schemes:
- UDID Card or Disability Certificate.
- Aadhaar Card.
- Income Certificate.
- Age Proof / BPL Card (where applicable).
- Bank account details.

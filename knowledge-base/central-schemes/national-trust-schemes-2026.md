## Introduction
The National Trust is a statutory body under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment, Government of India, set up for the welfare of persons with Autism, Cerebral Palsy, Intellectual Disability, and Multiple Disabilities.

## 1. NIRAMAYA Health Insurance Scheme
A health insurance scheme exclusively for PwDs providing coverage for a wide variety of medical needs without pre-insurance medical tests.
- **Coverage:** Up to Rs. 1 Lakh for OPD, hospitalization, medicines, dental care, surgeries, and therapies (physio, occupational, etc.).
- **Transportation:** Includes costs associated with care or treatment.
- **Eligibility:** Persons with valid disability certificate and UDID enrollment.

## 2. VIKAAS Daycare Scheme
Daycare centers run by certified organizations providing therapy, education, and social/life-skills training during daytime hours.
- **Benefits:** Special educators, therapy support (physio/occupational), counseling, and skill-building.
- **Eligibility:** Children/young persons (10+ years). Applicant must not be enrolled in GHARAUNDA or SAMARTH.

## 3. GHARAUNDA (Group-Home for Adults)
Provides safe, lifelong group-home living for adults with disabilities.
- **Benefits:** 24/7 care, medical access, vocational training, and social interaction in a structured environment.
- **Eligibility:** Adults (18+ years).

## 4. SAMARTH Respite & Residential Care
Offers residential or respite care, particularly for orphans, abandoned persons, or families in crisis.
- **Benefits:** Group-home style living, basic medical care, and support from special educators and vocational trainers.
- **Eligibility:** All ages (priority to those in crisis).

## 5. SAHYOGI Care-Associate Training Scheme
A training scheme to build a network of skilled caregivers (Care Associates).
- **Training:** Basic (3 months) and Advanced (6 months) courses covering first-aid, communication, and special needs support.
- **Eligibility:** Individuals aged 18-35 with minimum 8th-grade education.

## 6. BADHTE KADAM (Awareness & Inclusion)
Community sensitization program to promote social awareness and build inclusive societies.
- **Benefits:** Supports awareness drives, inclusive policies in schools/workplaces, and knowledge dissemination about disability rights.

## How to Apply
Applications for National Trust schemes can typically be made online through the [National Trust Portal](https://thenationaltrust.gov.in/) or via registered NGOs.

## Overview
The Pre Matric Scholarship For Students With Disabilities is a scheme provided by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities. This scheme aims to support students with disabilities in their pre-matric education. The scheme is open to students with disabilities who are pursuing their education in a government-recognized school.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance to students with disabilities
- Support for pre-matric education

## Eligibility criteria
- The student must have a disability
- The student must be pursuing pre-matric education
- The student must be studying in a government-recognized school

## Required documents
1. Disability certificate
2. Income certificate
3. Caste certificate (if applicable)

## How to apply
Step 1: Register on the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) website
Step 2: Fill in the application form and upload the required documents
Step 3: Submit the application form

## Important Dates (2025)
- **Scheme Open from:** 25-06-2025
- **Student Application Closed on:** 31-10-2025
- **Defective Application Verification Closed on:** 03-12-2025
- **Institute Verification Closed on:** 03-12-2025

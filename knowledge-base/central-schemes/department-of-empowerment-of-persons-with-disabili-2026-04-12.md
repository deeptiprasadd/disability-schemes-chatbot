## Overview
The Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) is a department under the Ministry of Social Justice and Empowerment that aims to empower persons with disabilities. The department provides various schemes and services to promote the rights and welfare of persons with disabilities.

## Benefits
- Empowerment of persons with disabilities
- Promotion of rights and welfare of persons with disabilities
- Provision of various schemes and services

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with disabilities
- Citizens of India

## Required documents
1. Disability certificate
2. Identity proof

Step by step instructions are not available, but applicants can visit the official website of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) for more information.

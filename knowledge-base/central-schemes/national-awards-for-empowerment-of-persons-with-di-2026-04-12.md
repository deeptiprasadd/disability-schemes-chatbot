## Overview
The National Awards for Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities is an award that aims to recognize and promote the empowerment of persons with disabilities.

## Benefits
- Recognition and promotion of empowerment of persons with disabilities

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with disabilities
- Citizens of India

## Required documents
1. Disability certificate
2. Identity proof

## How to apply
Step by step instructions are not available, but applicants can visit the official website of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) for more information.

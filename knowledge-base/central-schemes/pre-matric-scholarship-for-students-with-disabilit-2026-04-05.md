## Overview
The Pre Matric Scholarship For Students With Disabilities is a scheme provided by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities to support the education of students with disabilities. This scheme aims to promote inclusivity and provide financial assistance to students with disabilities. The scheme is open to students with disabilities who are pursuing their pre-matric education.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for pre-matric education
- Inclusive education for students with disabilities
- Promotion of education among students with disabilities

## Eligibility criteria
- Students with disabilities
- Pursuing pre-matric education
- Meeting the income criteria set by the government

## Required documents
1. Disability certificate
2. Income certificate
3. Proof of age
4. Proof of admission

## How to apply
Step 1: Register on the National Scholarship Portal (NSP)
Step 2: Fill in the application form
Step 3: Upload the required documents
Step 4: Submit the application

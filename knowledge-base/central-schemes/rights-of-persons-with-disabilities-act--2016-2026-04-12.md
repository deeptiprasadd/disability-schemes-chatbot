## Overview
The Rights of Persons with Disabilities Act, 2016 is an act that aims to promote the rights and welfare of persons with disabilities. The act provides for various provisions, including the recognition of 21 types of disabilities.

## Benefits
- Recognition of 21 types of disabilities
- Promotion of rights and welfare of persons with disabilities

## Eligibility criteria
- Persons with disabilities
- Citizens of India

## Required documents
1. Disability certificate
2. Identity proof

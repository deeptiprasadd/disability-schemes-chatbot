## Overview
The National Fund is a scheme initiated by the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) to provide financial assistance to persons with disabilities. The scheme aims to promote the welfare and empowerment of persons with disabilities. It provides assistance for education, employment, and other activities that help persons with disabilities to become self-reliant.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for education and employment
- Assistance for other activities that promote self-reliance
- Support for persons with disabilities to become independent

## Eligibility criteria
- The applicant must be a person with a disability
- The applicant must be a resident of India
- The applicant must meet the income criteria set by the government

## Required documents
1. Proof of disability
2. Proof of income
3. Proof of residence

## How to apply
Step 1: Visit the official website of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD)
Step 2: Fill out the application form and attach the required documents
Step 3: Submit the application form to the concerned authority

## Overview
The Assistance to Disabled Persons for Purchase/Fitting of Aids and Appliances (ADIP) scheme is an initiative of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) to provide assistance to persons with disabilities for the purchase and fitting of aids and appliances. The scheme aims to improve the quality of life and promote the independence of persons with disabilities. It provides financial assistance for the purchase of aids and appliances such as wheelchairs, hearing aids, and artificial limbs.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for the purchase and fitting of aids and appliances
- Assistance for the repair and maintenance of aids and appliances
- Support for persons with disabilities to become independent

## Eligibility criteria
- The applicant must be a person with a disability
- The applicant must be a resident of India
- The applicant must meet the income criteria set by the government

## Overview
The Scholarship scheme is an initiative of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD) to provide financial assistance to students with disabilities for their education. The scheme aims to promote the education and empowerment of students with disabilities. It provides financial assistance for tuition fees, books, and other educational expenses.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for tuition fees and other educational expenses
- Assistance for students with disabilities to pursue higher education
- Support for students with disabilities to become independent

## Eligibility criteria
- The applicant must be a student with a disability
- The applicant must be a resident of India
- The applicant must meet the income criteria set by the government

## Required documents
1. Proof of disability
2. Proof of income
3. Proof of residence
4. Proof of admission to an educational institution

## How to apply
Step 1: Visit the official website of the Department of Empowerment of Persons with Disabilities (DEPwD)

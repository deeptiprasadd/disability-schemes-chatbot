## Overview
myScheme is a National Platform that aims to offer one-stop search and discovery of the Government schemes. It provides an innovative, technology-based solution to discover scheme information based upon the eligibility of the citizen. The platform helps the citizen to find the right Government schemes for them.

## Benefits
- One-stop search and discovery of Government schemes
- Easy user interface to discover and apply for schemes
- Reduces time and effort of citizens in searching for appropriate government schemes

## Eligibility criteria
- Citizens can enter their demographic, income, and social details to find eligible schemes

## Required documents
1. Demographic details
2. Income details
3. Social details

## How to apply
Step 1: Enter user attributes such as demographic, income, social details, etc.
Step 2: myScheme finds the relevant schemes from hundreds of schemes for the user.
Step 3: The user can select from the list of eligible schemes and get more information from the dedicated scheme page with detailed knowledge, FAQs, and application process and traverse to the application URL to apply.

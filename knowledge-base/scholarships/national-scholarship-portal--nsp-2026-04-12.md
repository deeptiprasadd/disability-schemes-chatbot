## Overview
The National Scholarship Portal (NSP) is a digital platform that provides a range of scholarships to students in India. The portal aims to simplify the scholarship application process and make it more accessible to students. The NSP is available in multiple languages, including English, Hindi, Gujarati, and Tamil.

## Benefits
- Simplified scholarship application process
- Unique 14-digit One Time Registration (OTR) number for entire academic career
- Eliminates the need for registration in each academic year

## Eligibility criteria
- Students must have a valid Aadhaar or Aadhaar Enrollment number (EID)
- Students must unlock their biometric lock for authentication in Aadhaar

## Required documents
1. Aadhaar card
2. Aadhaar Enrollment number (EID)

## How to apply
Step 1: Visit the National Scholarship Portal (NSP) website
Step 2: Click on the "Apply now" button for One Time Registration (OTR)
Step 3: Enter your Aadhaar or Aadhaar Enrollment number (EID) to generate your OTR
Step 4: Fill out the application form and submit it

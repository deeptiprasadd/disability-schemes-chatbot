## Overview
The Legrand Empowering Scholarship Program is a scholarship scheme for students with disabilities, including girl students, LGBTQ+ students, differently-abled students, COVID-affected students, single-parent students, or orphan students. The scheme aims to provide financial assistance to students pursuing higher education. The program is open for students who have passed Class 12 and have secured a minimum of 70% marks in Class 10 and 12.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for higher education
- Empowerment of students with disabilities

## Eligibility criteria
- Open for girl students (including LGBTQ+, differently-abled girls, COVID-affected, single-parent, or orphan students)
- Admission in B.Tech, B.E., B.Arch., B.B.A., B.Com., or B.Sc.
- Must have passed Class 12
- Marks requirement: ≥70% in Class 10 & 12 (General)/≥60% (Special categories)
- Family income ≤ ₹5 lakh per annum

## Required documents
1. Proof of admission
2. Proof of marks in Class 10 and 12
3. Proof of family income
4. Valid disability certificate (if applicable)

## How to apply
Step 1: Visit the Buddy4Study portal at buddy4study.com
Step 2: Fill out the online application form
Step 3: Upload required documents
Step 4: Submit the application

- Empowerment of students with disabilities

- Family income ≤ ₹2.5 lakh per annum

## Overview
The Indus Towers Scholarship Program is a scholarship scheme for students with disabilities, including Indian nationals with benchmark disability ≥40%. The scheme aims to provide financial assistance to students pursuing higher education. The program is open for students who are domicile of Northeast states or studying in Institutes of National Importance.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance for higher education
- Empowerment of students with disabilities

## Eligibility criteria
- Indian nationals with benchmark disability ≥40%
- Category I: Domicile of Northeast states
- Category II: Students from the rest of India studying in Institutes of National Importance
- Valid disability certificate required

## Required documents

2. Proof of disability
3. Proof of domicile (if applicable)

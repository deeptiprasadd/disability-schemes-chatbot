## Overview
The National Old Age Pension Scheme is a government-funded program aimed at providing financial assistance to elderly individuals above 60 years of age, living below the poverty line. The scheme is implemented by the Department of Social Welfare, Uttar Pradesh.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance to elderly individuals
- Assistance for dignified living

## Eligibility criteria
- Age above 60 years
- Living below the poverty line

## Required documents
1. Age proof
2. Income certificate
3. Residence proof

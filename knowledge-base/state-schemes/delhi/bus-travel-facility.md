## Overview
The Delhi Transport Corporation (DTC) provides free travel facilities in DTC buses to persons with vision impairment. Additionally, a concession is provided for an escort/attendant under specific criteria.

## Benefits
- **Free Travel:** For persons with vision impairment in all DTC buses within Delhi.
- **Escort Concession:** 50% concession for the escort/attendant if the applicant has 100% disability.

## Eligibility Criteria
- **Disability:** Minimum 40% and above disability (for free travel).
- **Escort Criteria:** 100% disability required for escort concession.
- **Residence:** Must be a resident of Delhi.

## Required Documents
1. **SDM Identity Card:** Card issued by the Sub Divisional Magistrate (SDM).
2. **Residential Proof:** Document proving residence in Delhi.
3. **Disability Certificate:** Copy of valid disability certificate.
4. **Photographs:** Passport-size photographs.

## How to Apply
1. **Obtain SDM Card:** Applicant must first obtain the special card for PwDs from the concerned Sub Divisional Magistrate (SDM) office.
2. **Procure Form:** Visit any DTC depot and obtain the bus pass application form by paying ₹1.
3. **Submit Application:** Submit the filled form along with the SDM card and other documents at the respective DTC depot.
4. **Issuance:** The bus pass will be issued after verification of documents at the depot.

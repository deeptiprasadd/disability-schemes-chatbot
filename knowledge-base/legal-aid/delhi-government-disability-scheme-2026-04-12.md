## Overview
The Delhi Government Disability Scheme is a government initiative aimed at providing assistance to individuals with disabilities in the National Capital Territory of Delhi. The scheme provides various benefits, including financial assistance, to help individuals with disabilities lead a dignified life. The scheme is implemented by the Government of the National Capital Territory of Delhi.

## Benefits
- Financial assistance to individuals with disabilities
- Assistance for education and employment
- Assistance for medical treatment and rehabilitation

## Eligibility criteria
- The applicant must be a resident of the National Capital Territory of Delhi
- The applicant must have a disability of 40% or more
- The applicant must be below the poverty line

## Required documents
1. Proof of residence in the National Capital Territory of Delhi
2. Proof of disability (medical certificate)
3. Income certificate
4. Proof of age

## How to apply
Step 1: Obtain the application form from the nearest district social welfare office or download it from the official website.
Step 2: Fill in the application form and attach the required documents.
Step 3: Submit the application form to the nearest district social welfare office.
Step 4: The application will be verified and processed by the district social welfare office.
Step 5: The applicant will be informed about the status of their application.

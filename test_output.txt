Since your child cannot pursue education, the most relevant benefits are medical insurance and monthly pension.

**Financial Benefits:** **₹500/- per month** (Subsistence Allowance/Pension) for children with disabilities who require lifelong care and support. **Grant amount**: ₹2 lakhs (one-time grant) for setting up a home for children with disabilities.

**Who can apply:**
- Children with severe intellectual or physical disabilities
- Parents/guardians of children with disabilities

**How to apply:**
1. Visit the [National Trust Portal](https://thenationaltrust.gov.in/) and navigate to the "Schemes" section.
2. Click on "Subsidence Allowance (Pension)" and fill out the application form.
3. Submit the application along with required documents, such as proof of disability, age, identity, income, and financial need.

**Further Questions:** Can you please provide your child's age and percentage of disability?
